Storing passwords can be a tricky business, if someone gets a hold of them then they may just get access to all your accounts. An alternative would be to store hints for those passwords rather than the passwords themselves.

The hints themselves can be something very personal which make sense only to you. In that case, even if someone gets access to them, it will most likely mean nothing to them as they may not have the necessary context to guess the password. Adding a few special characters, or some digits will make password guessing even more difficult.

HintKeep is designed exactly for that. A store for password hints rather than passwords. You can create different accounts, pin them at the top to have easy access to them and for each have an associated hint and, if needed, some notes. You can always view your previously used password hints for each account. A simple application designed to cover something critical.
